## What is this?
**Pixel Experience is an AOSP/CAF based ROM, with Google apps included and all Pixel goodies (launcher, wallpapers, icons, fonts, bootanimation)
Our mission is to offer the maximum possible stability and security, along with essential features for the proper functioning of the device**

### What's working
* Boots
* RIL ( Data,SMS,Calls )
* VoLTE
* Fingerprint sensor
* WiFi
* Bluetooth
* Camera
* Camcorder
* Audio
* GPS
* Sensors
* Video Playback

2) Wipe data, cache and system

3) Flash lastest build , latest Magisk (Imp), gapps are included

4) Reboot your device once the installation is done
